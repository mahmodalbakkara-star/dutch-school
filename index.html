Discover the Netherlands and Its Culture

The Netherlands is not only a beautiful country filled with canals and windmills, but also a place with a rich and diverse culture. Dutch people are known for their openness, punctuality, organized lifestyle, and love for teamwork and cycling. By learning the Dutch language, you will be able to understand Dutch society more deeply and communicate confidently in daily life, whether for studying, working, or making new friends. In this section, you will explore Dutch traditions, holidays, popular foods, and everyday lifestyle in a simple and enjoyable way.
